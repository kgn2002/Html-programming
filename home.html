About Us

The Adi Shankara Institute of Engineering & Technology (ASIET), established in Kalady, aims to provide value-added technical education that fosters professional excellence and ethical values in students.Founded in 2001, ASIET is committed to the holistic development of its students. It is affiliated with A P J Abdul Kalam Technological University, approved by AICTE, and offers UG, PG, and PhD courses. Five of its streams (CE, CSE, ECE, EEE, and ME) are NBA accredited, reflecting its commitment to quality education. ASIET is the first self-financing engineering college in Kerala to receive ISO 9001:2008 certification.

Campus
