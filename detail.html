MCA

The ASIET Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program teaches students research and innovation skills. Students are immersed in cutting-edge technology and taught a transdisciplinary, application-oriented approach to tackling real-world challenges. The Department of Computer Application has a diverse group of motivated researchers in emerging fields like Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning, Internet of Things (IoT), Image Processing, Augmented Reality/Virtual Reality

  • Vision: Nurturing globally competent computing professionals with innovation, research, entrepreneurship skills, and social commitment.
  • Mission: Provide quality education with industry collaboration and excellence in the teaching-learning process.

Course Outcomes

Program Specific Outcomes (PSO)

  1. PSO1: Graduates will apply computing techniques and software technology in core application areas such as full-stack development and big data analysis.
  2. PSO2: Graduates will possess enhanced abilities in current trends in industry and research while maintaining a commitment to ethical and social values.

Programme Outcome (PO)

  1. PO1: Apply knowledge of computing fundamentals and mathematics to solve defined problems.
  2. PO2: Identify, formulate, and solve complex computing problems using fundamental principles.
  3. PO3: Design and evaluate solutions for complex computing problems considering various societal factors.
  4. PO4: Use research-based knowledge and methods to provide valid conclusions.
